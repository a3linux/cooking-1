# Main Dishes
## French Onion Soup
### Ingredients
```
Serves: 2 people
3tbsp   Unsalted Butter
1.5lbs  Onions  Red:Yellow:White:Shallot ratio of 1:1:1:3
1/2     Bay Leaf
1/4cup  Dry Sherry
1qt     Chicken or Beef Stock
1sprig  Fresh Thyme
        Rustic Bread (Stale is better)
1       Baguette that is wide and flat enough to fit your container
1clove  Garlic
1lb     Lots of Gruyere Cheese
```
